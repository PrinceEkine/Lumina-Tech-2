Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through our contact forms, booking systems, and portal registrations. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and project details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is primarily used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. This includes coordinating project milestones, sending booking confirmations, and communicating updates about our collaborative progress.

3. Data Security

Lumina Tech employs state-of-the-art encryption and security protocols (including Supabase RLS policies) to ensure your information remains protected from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use verified third-party services for hosting, authentication, and communication. These partners are vetted to ensure they meet our high standards for data privacy and security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. Simply contact our privacy team at privacy@luminatech.com.

Company Leave Policy

1. Available Days

  • Annual Leave: 20 days per calendar year.
  • Sick Leave: 10 days (medical certificate required for >2 days).
  • Casual Leave: 5 days for urgent personal matters.

2. Application Process

  • Submit request via the "Apply for Leave" button above.
  • Apply at least 2 weeks in advance for planned annual leave.
  • Ensure your tasks are handed over or scheduled before departure.

3. Approval Guidelines

  • Approvals are based on team capacity and project deadlines.
  • Your manager will review and approve/reject within 48 hours.
  • Check the Status column in your history for updates.
